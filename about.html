About Us

We started as three engineers tired of 2am pager duty.

ABC Technologies was founded on a simple belief: most outages are preventable, and most software doesn't have to be fragile. Today we operate infrastructure for clients across finance, retail, and healthcare — but the standard hasn't changed.

Our Values

What we optimize for.

01

Reliability over speed

We'll take an extra sprint to build something that doesn't page you at 3am.

02

Ownership through delivery

The engineers who design a system are the ones who operate it in production.

03

Transparency by default

Clients see the same dashboards and incident reports our own team does.

04

Automate the repeatable

If a human does it twice, we build a pipeline so they don't do it a third time.
